    \begin{abstract}
        Red Blood Cells are present in the human body and their purpose is to deliver oxygen to the human body while giving carbon dioxide for humans to exhale. The purpose of this paper is to show that Red Blood Cells can maintain an equilibrium state if a certain condition is met. The goal of this work is to show that Red Blood Cell population in the human body will maintain in an equilibrium state under a specific condition. This condition is where $\gamma = 1$.
